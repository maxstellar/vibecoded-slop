// mission

Lead by example. Mentor the rookies. Ship the foundations.

01

Set the bar

We don’t coast on seniority. Every season we re-earn our spot at the top of the board.

02

Mentor by default

Half our PRs are on other teams’ repos. If a rookie is stuck, our DMs are open and our review comments are kind.

03

Document everything

The Game’s playbook lives in our wiki. We treat docs as code, and we treat code as documentation for future-us.

04

Keep the lights on
