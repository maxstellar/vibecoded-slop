// mission

Measure stuff. Visualize it. Make it useful.

01

Sensors over speeches

Anyone can post a climate take. We’d rather wire up a sensor, log a year of data, and let the chart do the talking.

02

Local first

We pick projects that help our own school, our own town, or our own bedroom first. Scale comes after “does it work.”

03

Open data

Every reading we collect goes into a public bucket with a permissive license. If a researcher wants it, it’s a curl away.

04

Optimism, evidenced

We’re not doomers. We just want to see the line on the chart go down, and we’re willing to build the chart ourselves.
