// mission

Build cool stuff. Help each other. Win the season.

01

Ship weekly

Every Friday, every member ships something — a feature, a demo, a write-up, a sticker design. Momentum > perfection.

02

Open by default

All our code lives on GitHub. All our docs are public. If a teammate is stuck, the answer is one DM (or one PR) away.

03

Learn loud

We post our wins and our face-plants. Every bug we squash becomes a lesson the next person on the team doesn’t have to relearn from scratch.

04

Stay green

The contribution graph is our scoreboard. We keep it green — and we keep it kind. No gatekeeping, no “just google it.”
