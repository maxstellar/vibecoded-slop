// mission

Take the work seriously. Take ourselves, not so much.

01

Stay crisp

Small commits. Clear PRs. Nobody on this team has ever pushed a 4,000-line refactor on a Friday afternoon. (We’ve been tempted.)

02

Lead with a joke

Standups start with a bad pun. Demos end with a worse one. It’s a feature, not a bug — we’ve checked.

03

Ship the silly thing

The dumb idea is often the good idea in disguise. We build it, we ship it, and then we decide if it was dumb.

04

Be a good salad

No single ingredient carries the bowl. Every teammate brings their crunch. (Yes we’re still going with this.)
